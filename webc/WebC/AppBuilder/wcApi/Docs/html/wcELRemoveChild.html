wcBOOL wcELRemoveChild

(wcEL parent, wcEL child)

Remove a child element from its parent.

Documentation

Remove a child element from its parent. The child element must be contained by the parent. The child and parent are unlinked. The child is not deleted, it may be deleted or inserted into another container.

Parameters:
wcEL - Parent - The parent "container" element.
wcEL - Child - The child element to remove.
Returns:
wcTRUE if the arguments non NULL. It is the callers responsibilty to ensure Child is a direct child of parent.
