void wcImageGraphEndDraw

(wcEL ImageElement)

Close a sequence of draw commands and update the display.

Documentation

wcImageGraphEndDraw updates the screen from the image pallet that was just drawn on and then restores the image's original content.

Parameters:
wcEL - ImageElement - An image element that is the background canvas for our draw operations and was previously an argument to wcImageGraphBeginDraw.
Returns:
nothing
